Welcome to my webpage!

I am a former attorney who is currently studying coding at Interface Web School in Omaha and through FreeCodeCamp.com. My goals are to become a Full-Stack Web Developer and a Mobile App Developer. I am specifically interested in developing websites for small businesses, including accounting and law firms run by solo practitioners.

I believe a developer should take responsibility for her or his code from start to finish. That is why I test every aspect of a website to insure that all parts work before turning over the keys to the client.

I also believe that both the developer and the client have rights and responsibilities in the development of a site. I have drafted a list of such rights and responsibilities, which you can view here.
